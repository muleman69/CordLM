CordLM Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CordLM collects and processes the following data to provide our services:

  • Discord Server IDs where the bot is installed
  • Channel IDs where commands are used
  • Message content for summarization purposes
  • User IDs for command attribution and premium features

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing channel summarization services
  • Improving our AI models and service quality
  • Managing premium subscriptions
  • Generating analytics and usage statistics

3. Data Retention

Message content is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored. Server and channel IDs are retained while the bot is installed. Premium subscription data is retained for accounting purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection by removing the bot

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Discord API for bot functionality
  • AI language models for summarization
  • Payment processors for premium subscriptions

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
